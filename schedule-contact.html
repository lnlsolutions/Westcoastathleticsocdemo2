Owner + Head Coach

Christian Wadwa

Christian Wadwa is a Nationally Certified Personal Trainer with more than a decade of coaching experience since 2013. He specializes in weight management, corrective exercise, strength and conditioning, athletic performance, and combat sports development.

A lifelong martial artist, he holds a Brown Belt in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, is an active Muay Thai and MMA competitor, a USA Boxing Certified Coach, and Head Coach to amateur combat athletes across Southern California.
